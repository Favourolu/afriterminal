Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 11 March 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Welcome to AfriTerminal — African Capital Markets Intelligence. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform. By using AfriTerminal, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

AfriTerminal is committed to transparency. We are a financial intelligence tool built specifically for African markets, and we take your privacy seriously.

01 — WHO WE ARE

AfriTerminal is an African financial markets dashboard providing real-time data on stocks, foreign exchange, cryptocurrency, commodities, and macroeconomic indicators across Africa.

The platform was conceived and founded by David Awoyemi, with technical development and engineering aided by Oluwemimo Favour.

02 — INFORMATION WE COLLECT

AfriTerminal is designed to be privacy-first. We collect minimal data necessary to operate the platform:

  • Portfolio data — positions and buy prices you enter are stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to our servers.
  • Price alerts — alert conditions you set are stored locally in your browser session only.
  • Usage data — we may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics such as page views and feature usage to improve the platform. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Search queries — stock and asset searches are processed locally and are not logged or stored.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or financial account details unless you explicitly provide them through a registration or contact form.

03 — THIRD-PARTY DATA SOURCES

AfriTerminal connects to the following external APIs to provide live market data. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • ExchangeRate API (open.er-api.com) — live foreign exchange rates
  • CoinGecko API (api.coingecko.com) — live cryptocurrency prices
  • World Bank Open Data API (api.worldbank.org) — macroeconomic indicators
  • African Markets (african-markets.com) — African stock exchange listings

When your browser loads AfriTerminal, it makes requests to these APIs. Your IP address may be visible to these services as part of standard web traffic. We do not share any personal data with these providers.

04 — HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Any information processed by AfriTerminal is used solely to:

  • Display real-time and historical market data relevant to African exchanges
  • Render your portfolio positions and P&L calculations
  • Trigger price alerts based on conditions you have set
  • Improve platform performance and user experience

We do not sell, rent, or trade any user data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

05 — DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

AfriTerminal stores portfolio and alert data in your browser's local session memory. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device — it is not uploaded to any AfriTerminal server
  • Closing or refreshing the browser will reset session data unless persistent storage is implemented
  • We use HTTPS for all external API calls to ensure data in transit is encrypted

While we implement reasonable technical measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage users not to enter sensitive financial credentials into the platform.

06 — COOKIES

AfriTerminal does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We may use minimal session cookies solely for platform functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect platform performance.

07 — YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any analytics collection
  • Be informed about how your data is used

Since AfriTerminal stores data locally on your device, you can delete your data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage and session data.

08 — CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

AfriTerminal is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the platform, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

09 — CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the platform evolves. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
